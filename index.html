About

MICHAEL LYNN

IFBB Pro. NYC Trainer. Obsessed with Results.

I've competed at the highest level of natural bodybuilding, earning my IFBB Pro card and standing on stage at the New York Pro, Chicago Pro, and Tampa Pro. But my real education came from 10+ years in the gym, training real people with real lives.

Based in Manhattan, I run 40 to 50 training sessions every single week. My clients range from first-timers who've never touched a barbell to competitive bodybuilders chasing a pro card. What they all have in common: they're serious about change.

I built my reputation one result at a time. Just dedication, discipline, and determination the same three D's I've lived by my entire career.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, direct answers to common questions regarding the coaching system and protocols.

Is this program right for complete beginners?

Absolutely. While I coach IFBB Pro competitors, the majority of my clients are everyday people who are serious about making a real change. If you're willing to put in the work, I'll build the exact plan to get you there.

Do I need to be in New York to work with you?

No. My online coaching clients are based all over the world. If you're in New York, we can train in person. Anywhere else, everything runs remotely with the same level of coaching quality.

Do I need a fully equipped gym?

A commercial gym is recommended, but not required. If you're training at home, I'll build the entire program around what you have available.

How quickly will I see results?

If you follow the plan consistently, most clients notice visible changes within 2 to 3 weeks. Major transformations typically happen by weeks 10 to 12.

How do I book an appointment?

Fill out the contact form on this page and I'll get back to you within 24 hours to schedule your free intro call. Online clients can also book directly through the scheduling tool.
